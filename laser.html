Laser Hair Removal

What Is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal uses a focused beam of light to target the pigment in hair follicles, breaking them down and preventing future hair growth.

How Does It Work?

The laser targets the pigment in the hair, heating and destroying the follicle without harming surrounding skin. This stops the hair from regrowing.
