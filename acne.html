What is Acne?

Acne is a common skin condition that affects the hair follicles and oil (sebaceous) glands. It occurs when the hair follicles become clogged with a mix of oil (sebum) and dead skin cells, leading to the development of blackheads and whiteheads, also known as comedones.

How We Treat Acne?

At Estemica Clinic, we treat not only the symptoms of acne but also its underlying causes. Our acne treatments focus on cleansing the skin, removing blackheads, and reducing inflammation, helping you achieve clear and healthy skin.

Each treatment starts with a deep cleanse to remove excess oil and impurities, followed by blackhead extraction. After that, we move on to the main phase of the treatment to ensure long-lasting results. Includes various methods for solving aesthetic problems and improving skin health using modern equipment.
