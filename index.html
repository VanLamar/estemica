About us

Estemica Estetic Clinic, located in Montreal and Laval, offers modern cosmetology and advanced skin care treatments. We specialize in anti-aging and anti-acne facials, laser treatments, permanent hair removal, peelings, and face massages. Using only premium, high-quality cosmetics and brands such as Thesera, DP Dermaceuticals, Dermaceuticals MG-Collection, and PROMOITALIA, we ensure the highest standards of care. Our goal is to help you achieve outstanding results, maintaining rejuvenated and glowing beauty with the best care for your skin.

Yulia Kakabadze

Founder, medical professional, esthetician

Yulia is an internationally trained medical professional with a medical degree from Russia and expertise in dermatology and pathology, gaining extensive experience in both fields. After moving to Canada, advanced studies in Biomedical Technology led to a career in esthetics. As an expert in laser treatments, microneedling, facials, peels, and face massage, Yulia now focuses on delivering personalized treatments that help clients achieve radiant, glowing skin, believing that healthy skin enhances confidence and happiness.

Certified in laser hair removal, laser photo rejuvenation, and microneedling, offering save and effective solutions for hair removal, skin concerns, and acne scars. Committed to sharing a passion for beauty and delivering exceptional results.
