What is Rosacea?

Rosacea is a chronic skin condition that causes redness, visible small blood vessels, and sometimes bumps that look like acne. It comes and goes, with flare-ups followed by periods of calm.Â Managing rosacea requires ongoing care to control symptoms and prevent flare-ups.

How Do We Treat Rosacea at Estemica Clinic?

At Estemica Clinic, we offer advanced treatments for rosacea using laser therapy and microneedling. Laser therapy targets the small blood vessels, reducing redness and visible veins, while microneedling helps improve skin texture and stimulates collagen production, aiding in skin recovery and reducing inflammation. Both treatments are non-invasive and tailored to suit your skin's needs, providing effective results with minimal downtime.
